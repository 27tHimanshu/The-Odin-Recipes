Jalebi

A popular Indian snack with a crispy, triangular pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons gram flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • A pinch of saffron strands
  • Oil for frying
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • A few saffron strands
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

    • Recipe

  • Mix flours, baking soda, and yogurt into a smooth batter. Add saffron and let it ferment for a few hours.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Pour the batter in a squeeze bottle or a cloth cone.
  • Squeeze the batter into hot oil in pretzel shapes, frying until golden.
  • In a separate pan, prepare sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water, then adding saffron and cardamom.
  • Dip fried jalebis in the sugar syrup for a few seconds before serving.
