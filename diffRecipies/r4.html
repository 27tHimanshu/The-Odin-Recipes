Gulab Jamun

A popular Indian snack with a crispy, triangular pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • A pinch of baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • A few tablespoons milk
  • Oil for frying
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • A few drops of rose water
  • A pinch of cardamom powder

    • Recipe

  • In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and ghee. Gradually add milk and knead the mixture into a soft, smooth dough. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water and adding rose water and cardamom.
  • Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth, crack-free balls.
  • Heat oil in a pan and fry the dough balls on low heat until they turn golden brown.
  • Drain and immediately immerse them in the sugar syrup.
  • Let the gulab jamuns soak in the syrup for a few hours before serving.
