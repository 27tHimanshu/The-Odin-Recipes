Biryani

A popular Indian snack with a crispy, triangular pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, mutton, or vegetables
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish

    • Recipe

  • Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes. Cook until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  • Marinate meat or vegetables in yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric, and red chili powder.
  • In a large pan, heat ghee and oil. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
  • Add meat or vegetables and cook until partially done. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  • In a separate dish, dissolve saffron strands in warm milk.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice and meat/vegetable mixture in a large pot. Drizzle saffron milk.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes. Garnish with coriander and mint leaves.
